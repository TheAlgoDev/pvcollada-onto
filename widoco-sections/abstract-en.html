The PV-Collada Ontology (pvc:) is an OWL 2 DL ontology that formalises the three-layer data model of the PV-Collada 2.0 XML schema: a product library of typed component specifications, an electrical circuit topology, and a 3-D physical layout derived from COLLADA scene-graph geometry.

The ontology is a subdomain module of MDS-Onto (Materials Data Science Ontology, domain builtenv) and is formally aligned with MDS-Onto v0.3.1.31 through rdfs:subClassOf, skos:exactMatch, skos:closeMatch, and rdfs:seeAlso links. It covers photovoltaic modules, inverters, MPPT channels, string combiners, cables, power optimizers, racks, trackers, tables, posts, and terrain meshes, together with their circuit and physical interconnections.
